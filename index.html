Features

Vajra Linux offers a wide range of features to cater to diverse user needs:

• Lightweight and fast, perfect for older hardware.

• Supports more than 10 Indian languages for accessibility.

• Customizable layouts inspired by Windows and macOS.

• Preloaded with educational and developer-friendly tools.

• Enhanced security and privacy features for modern users.

About the Developer

Vajra Linux is the brainchild of Hrishikesh Sachin Kelkar, a visionary 16-year-old programmer and Linux enthusiast from India. Hrishikesh has been coding since 2019, gaining expertise in programming, cybersecurity, and Rust development. His dedication to open-source technology led to the creation of Vajra Linux, a distribution tailored for accessibility, efficiency, and innovation.

Hrishikesh’s achievements include winning a national-level hackathon and actively contributing to open-source communities. Currently, he is working on cutting-edge cybersecurity projects and exploring the depths of Rust to develop advanced tools and systems. With Vajra Linux, he aims to empower users, particularly in India, by providing a robust and versatile operating system.
