About the Developer

Vajra Linux is developed by Hrishikesh Sachin Kelkar, a 16-year-old programmer, Linux enthusiast, and a passionate advocate for open-source technology. Hrishikesh has been coding since 2019 and has six years of experience, focusing on Linux systems, cybersecurity, and programming in Rust. A national-level hackathon winner, he is committed to creating impactful, user-friendly software solutions. His vision is to make Linux more accessible and efficient for users around the globe, particularly in India.

In addition to developing Vajra Linux, Hrishikesh actively engages in the fields of cybersecurity and system optimization, reflecting his dedication to leveraging technology for societal benefit.
